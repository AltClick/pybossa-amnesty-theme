URGENT ACTIONS VISUALISED

Explore the results of our first Amnesty Decoders project.

The report

In just under 2 months, 8000 digital volunteers from more than 150 countries spent over 1000 hours to help us decode and verify 2443 Urgent Actions and identify trends emerging from our massive archive of data.

The findings will help us to be more effective at supporting people who face human rights abuses in the future. Below you can interact with some of the results our Decoders have helped us analyse.
